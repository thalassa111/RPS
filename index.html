Rock Paper Scissor

According to Wikipedia's website,

The players may count aloud to three, or speak the name of the game (e.g. "Rock! Paper! Scissors!"), either raising one hand in a fist and swinging it down with each syllable or holding it behind their back. They then "throw" or "shoot" by extending it towards their opponent. Variations include a version where players throw immediately on the third count (thus throwing on the count of "Scissors!"), or a version where they shake their hands three times before "throwing".

My own version

Click "New Game" to start spinning the reels, make your pick on the left side, rock, paper or scissor. After your pick, the reels will stopp spinning, the arrow in the right side marks the AI pick. Yes, you play three at the same time, and the outcome of each reel will be shown on the top right side. To play again, press "New Game". History on the bottom right will show the outcome from last time. Have fun!
